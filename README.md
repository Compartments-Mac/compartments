## What is Compartments?  

Compartments Mac is an advanced anatomy tool designed to provide an in-depth look at the muscular, vascular, and fascial compartments of the human body. This application is ideal for medical students, healthcare professionals, and anatomy enthusiasts seeking accurate and detailed anatomical references.  

The software covers a wide range of body regions including arms, legs, hands, feet, and the abdomen. It provides detailed visualizations of individual compartments, highlighting structures relevant for understanding conditions like compartment syndrome, trauma injuries, and post-surgical recovery.  

With interactive diagrams, users can zoom into specific compartments, explore layers of muscle and fascia, and examine clinical scenarios related to fractures, hematomas, and nerve compression. The app also explains compartment-related medical conditions, providing practical knowledge for both learning and clinical practice.  

Key Features Include:  
- Extensive 2D and 3D anatomical compartment illustrations  
- Detailed coverage of upper and lower limbs, hands, feet, and abdominal regions  
- Educational insights into trauma, compartment syndrome, and other clinical conditions  
- Interactive interface with zoom, layer toggling, and labeling options  
- Optimized for macOS on both Intel and Apple Silicon (M1/M2/M3)  
- Useful for students, medical practitioners, and researchers  

Compartments Mac combines visual precision with educational depth, offering a reliable tool for studying anatomy and understanding the implications of compartment-related medical conditions.
